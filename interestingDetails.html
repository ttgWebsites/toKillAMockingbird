Interesting Details

  • Unlike some famous novels and works of literature that took years or decades to be discovered and acclaimed by many, To Kill a Mockingbird had success almost overnight. It went on to win a Pulitzer Prize as well.
  • A very famous film version of the movie was made just two years after the book was published; it had great success as well, and is still well received by many.
  • The author, Harper Lee, had not published a novel before writing this one. To this day, she has yet to publish any other novels, and only published a few short essays. This novel is the literary definition of a one-hit-wonder.
