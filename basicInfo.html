Basic Info

  • Title
  • To Kill a Mockingbird
  • Author
  • Harper Lee
  • Published
  • published in 1960 by J. B. Lippincott
  • Genre
  • Coming of age, drama.
  • Setting
  • The novel is set in Alabama in a fictional town known as Maycomb. It takes place over the course of two years from 1933 to 1935.
  • Point Of View
  • The novel is written in the first person from the character Scout's point of view, she recounts the story several years after it occurred.
  • Conflict
  • The main conflict centers around a young girl, Scout, and boy, Jem. The boys innocence is put to the test as they begin to encounter the vile side of human nature and the reality of the adult world. The two must work to maintain faith in humanity, however it is very difficult due to the injustices they witness in the community they live in.
